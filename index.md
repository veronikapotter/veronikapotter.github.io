I am a Computer Science PhD Cantitidate at Northeastern University (Boston, MA, USA) advised by [Stephen Intille](https://ccis.northeastern.edu/home/intille/). 
The first two years of my PhD were funded by the Khoury Fellowship. 

Broadly speaking I'm interested in problems (1) using technology to improve **individuals** lives and (2) accurately and effectively communicating data. 
Currently, I primarily work researching how data from  wearable sensors can be used to detect activities **individuals** or researchers analyzing health survaillance study data care about. 

I graduated the University of Vermont in January 2022 with degrees in Mathematics (BS) and Computer Science (BA) with a minor in writing. 
My undergraduate mathematics research was advised by [Taylor Dupuy]() and my thesis on computer science education was advised by [Lisa Dion]().
